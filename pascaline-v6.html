COMPLEMENT / SUBTRACT

How to Subtract (100 - 10)

1. Enter 100 using the wheels and press "Enter / Add".
2. Set the wheels to 10 (the number you want to subtract).
3. Press "Subtraction Mode". The screen will change to show the complement view.
4. Press "Enter / Add". The machine adds the complement of 10.
5. Press "Subtraction Mode" again to turn it off. The correct result (90) will be revealed.
